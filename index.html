Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2023

Welcome to SDAMCHUB! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our fitness tracking and analytics application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Note: SDAMCHUB is designed with privacy in mind. We minimize data collection and prioritize storing your information locally on your device whenever possible.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data

We may collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register with the application or use its features, such as:

  • Name and email address
  • Age, height, weight, and fitness goals
  • Profile information and preferences

Fitness and Activity Data

To provide our core fitness tracking services, we collect and store:

  • Workout details (type, duration, intensity)
  • Exercise repetitions, sets, and weights
  • Activity metrics (steps, distance, calories burned)
  • Performance progress over time
  • Health data you choose to connect (with your explicit permission)

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our application, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • Usage statistics and feature interactions
  • Performance data and error reports (to help us improve the app)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our fitness tracking and analytics services
  • To personalize your fitness experience and provide relevant recommendations
  • To generate insights and visualizations about your activity patterns and progress
  • To send you administrative information, such as updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • To monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities to enhance user experience
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues

3. Data Storage and Local Processing

SDAMCHUB is designed to prioritize your privacy:

  • Local Storage: Your fitness data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • Optional Cloud Sync: Cloud backup is available as an optional feature, which you can enable or disable in settings
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect essential information needed to provide our services

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share information we have collected about you in certain situations:

With Your Consent

We may disclose your personal information with your consent, such as when you choose to share your fitness achievements on social media.

Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party vendors who perform services for us, such as:

  • Cloud storage providers (if you enable cloud sync)
  • Analytics services (in anonymized, aggregated form)
  • Customer support platforms

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information where required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate security measures designed to protect the security of your personal information. However, please remember that we cannot guarantee that the internet itself is 100% secure.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: You can update or correct your information in the app settings
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal data
  • Export: You can export your fitness data from the application

7. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
